About TMC Ministries

TMC is a ministry on a mission: to raise sound that saves souls and builds Kingdom armies. Through music, rap, worship, and Spirit-filled content, we expand God’s Kingdom across the world. Messianic Rap – Led by Minister Bruno, gifted rappers deliver Biblical truth over powerful beats, inspiring believers and challenging hearts. Worship – Guided by Edeni Rabbi, TMC’s visionary, our worship songs and devotionals lead listeners into deep encounters with God. At TMC, every beat, lyric, and melody is a tool to glorify God, strengthen His people, and reach every corner of the earth with His message. Join us as we create, worship, and move in faith to advance the Kingdom.
